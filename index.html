BIOGRAPHY

Artist and designer living in Paris One of the brightest representatives of contemporary art in Belarus and Europe, is rapidly and confidently gaining popularity in painting and fashion design, as well as in collaboration with major world's brands: Lexus,Sber Bank, Inglot cosmetics and Swiss watch Beam. Mio's works are in large private collections in France,Cyprus,Turkey,Germany,Ukraine,Russia and Belarus in the same collections with such artists as Malevich, Kandinsky,Richter,Andy Warhol, Banksy and Crypto Punk.

Cooperation with the catalog NFT GENERATION ART SHOW, Helsinki. Collaboration with the gallery ARTSI in Paris. In his works, Mio takes simple,understandable forms and things, showing them through the eyes of a naive child who is not afraid of bright colors and uneven lines. The artist says:"My art-works the world in the child's eyes; like through rose-colored glasses". Children are surprised by many things and situations that we adults do not notice and pass by them.Children are fascinated by colors, sounds and words in which we find nothing special.
